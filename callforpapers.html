Submission Guidelines

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.
The following paper categories are welcome:

Regular papers: 9 pages, 2-column format, including references.

Short papers: 4 pages, 2-column format, including references

The formatting requirements are equivalent to those of the main conference. For LaTeX users: unzip acmart.zip, make, and use sample-sigconf.tex as a template; Additional information about formatting and style files is available online at
https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template.
